RESERVE BANK OF INDIA
Ref: RBI/2026-27/42
Date: June 19, 2026

Subject: Mandatory Update to KYC Re-verification Process

In view of recent cybersecurity incidents, all regulated entities are directed to implement the following measures:
1. All flagged accounts must undergo full KYC re-verification.
2. Implement a secondary biometric authentication system.
3. Banks must ensure strict compliance with data localization norms.

Deadline for compliance: August 15, 2026.
